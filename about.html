Maria Obukhovskaya, also known as Mya, is an international model, originally comes from Vladivostok, Russia. She studied at Russian Far Eastern Federal University. Mya's native language is Russian, she speaks English and Vietnamese fluently and she is also good at Chinese.

Mya has started her modeling career at age of 17 doing some local advertising. At the age of 20, she won "Miss Sweet Pacha 2010" competition in Sitges, Spain. Few years later she went to Vietnam as an exchange student and there she kept working as a model. Mya signed contracts with two leading model agencies, "Venus" in Hanoi and "Elite Model Management" in Ho Chi Minh. She also works with Tay Model Management.

She has experience working with a variety of local designers and companies as well as taking part in fashion shows of such famous brands like Rimowa, Swarovski, Escada, Fcuk, Talbot Runhof, Adidas, Bonia, Valentino Rudi, Asoen, Carlo Rino, Sugar and Spice, Milano etc.

Mya takes pictures for different ads and catalogues which are published in Vietnam, America and France. Staying in Vietnam she keeps performing, shooting for many local and international magazines and billboards. She was acknowledged as one of the Top People of Vladivostok by the Planet magazine.

Mya is very professional when it comes to work and she is completely devoted to it. She knows all about modeling and fashion and knows the way to smile with her eyes.

Being a very talented girl, Mya succeeded not only in modeling. At the same time she is also an excellent dancer, a professional singer and an active member of Vietnam's higher society.

Mya is very cheerful, friendly and open-minded. Apart from work she likes cooking for her family and friends, travelling, learning foreign languages, meeting people, trying out everything new. The biggest hope she carries is the whole World to explore her talents.
